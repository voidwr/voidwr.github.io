Who Am I?

I'm a cybersecurity enthusiast passionate about penetration testing, CTF challenges, and vulnerability research. My journey in cybersecurity started with a curiosity about how systems work and how to secure them. I dedicate my time to learning new techniques, documenting my findings, and sharing knowledge with the community.

Through platforms like HackTheBox, TryHackMe, and CTF competitions, I continuously try to sharpen my skills in areas including network penetration testing, web application security, privilege escalation, and cryptography.

I'm by far not an expert in all the areas of cybersecurity, but I am committed to continuous learning and improvement.

This portfolio serves as a documentation of my learning journey and a resource for others interested in cybersecurity. Each writeup represents a challenge overcome and a lesson learned.
